Frequently Asked Questions

What is the SMS subscription service?

Our SMS subscription service allows you to receive updates about new books, special promotions, and restocks directly to your mobile phone.

How do I subscribe to the SMS service?

You can subscribe by visiting our subscription page and entering your mobile number and preferences.

Is there a cost for the SMS subscription?

Our SMS subscription service is free of charge, but standard SMS rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Can I unsubscribe from the SMS service?

Yes, you can unsubscribe at any time by following the instructions provided in the SMS messages.

What types of books can I receive updates about?

You can choose from various categories such as Fiction, Non-Fiction, Science, and more when subscribing.

How can I contact customer support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact our support team through the contact page on our website.
